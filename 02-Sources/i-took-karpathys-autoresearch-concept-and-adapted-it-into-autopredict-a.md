## Summary
AutoPredict is a research framework adapted from Karpathy's Autoresearch concept specifically designed for evaluating, backtesting, and iteratively improving prediction market trading agents. It assesses agents on forecast quality, calibration, execution metrics, and risk-adjusted returns while supporting domain specialists across finance, weather, and politics. This framework directly addresses the user's interest in AI-driven trading strategies and Polymarket prediction markets by providing a structured approach to agent improvement through evaluation, mutation, and selection loops.
