# AI Elevates Product Manager Role in Tech Development

## Source
[Original Link](https://x.com/palis/status/2036883260513673610?s=46)

## Summary
This social media post discusses how AI is changing the value distribution across tech roles, arguing that product managers become more powerful as design and coding become commoditized. The key takeaway is that while top engineers and designers remain valuable for specialized problem-solving, mid-level PMs have inherent advantages in ideation and critical thinking that AI can't fully replace. For someone building multi-agent systems, this highlights the importance of designing workflows that leverage AI for execution while preserving human strategic oversight in areas like trading strategy development.
