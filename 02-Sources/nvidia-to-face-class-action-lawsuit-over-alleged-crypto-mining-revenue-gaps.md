# Nvidia to Face Class Action Lawsuit Over Alleged Crypto Mining Revenue Gaps

## Source
[Original Link](https://decrypt.co/362410/nvidia-class-action-lawsuit-alleged-crypto-mining-revenue-gaps)

## Summary
This article reports on a class action lawsuit against Nvidia alleging the company misled investors about revenue from crypto mining during the 2017-2018 cryptocurrency boom. The key takeaway is that Nvidia may have concealed the extent of its crypto-related revenue, which could impact investor confidence and stock analysis models. For someone building AI trading systems, this highlights the importance of accurate revenue attribution in stock analysis and the potential risks when companies have undisclosed exposure to volatile sectors like cryptocurrency mining.
