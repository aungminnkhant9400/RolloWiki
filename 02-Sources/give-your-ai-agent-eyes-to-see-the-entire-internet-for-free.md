# Give your ai agent eyes to see the entire internet for free

## Source
[Original Link](https://x.com/GithubProjects/status/2037839641215398223?s=20)

## Summary
This is a command-line interface tool that enables AI agents to search and read content from multiple internet platforms without API fees. The key takeaway is that it provides free access to real-time data from Twitter, Reddit, YouTube, GitHub, and Chinese platforms like Bilibili and XiaoHongShu. For someone building OpenClaw multi-agent systems, this offers a practical solution for gathering market intelligence and social sentiment data that could enhance trading strategies and prediction market analysis.
